Computing Related Legislation


Computers are something that everyone uses in their day to day life and many peoples entire lifes revolve around them as well as the internet. Because of this it is very important that what we do on our computers and online is safe for us as well as other people. Over the years plenty of important legislations and laws have come out concerning computers.

Some of the most important legislations concerning computing includes:

  • The Data Protection act 1998
  • The Computer Misuse act 1990
  • The Copyright Design and Patents act 1988
  • The Regulation of Investigatory Powers
