The Regulation of Investigatory Powers


The Regulation Of Investigatory Powers allows certain groups to access digital communication held by any online body (person or company) and carry out digital surveillance.

The main groups that the RIPA is relevant to are and some examples:

  • Internet Service Providers.
    • BT.
    • Virgin Media.
    • Sky.
    • Talk Talk.
  • Buisnesses and facilities that allow for digital communication and storage.
    • Any Buisness that provide digital communication or the storage of digital data and have the hardware and software necassery for storage of a digital communication.
  • A range of public bodies who can access digital communication or stored data under certain circumstances.
    • Scottish Environment Protection Agency
    • Office of Fair Trading
    • Serious Fraud Office


RIPA means that anyones browsing data can be accessed by these bodies if it is deemed appropriate.
